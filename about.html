Meet the Founder

Santosh Maurya

Founder, Lead Photographer & Videographer

Hi, I'm Santosh Maurya—the creative force behind thehitchedstories. My journey in wedding photography began with a simple belief: every love story deserves to be captured with authenticity, emotion, and cinematic beauty.

thehitchedstories was born from my passion for storytelling through the lens. What started as a one-man vision has grown into a talented team of photographers, cinematographers, and editors who share the same dedication to excellence and creativity.

We've had the privilege of capturing hundreds of weddings across India and beyond—each one unique, each one special. Our approach combines editorial artistry with documentary authenticity, ensuring your wedding memories are preserved beautifully for generations to come.

— Santosh Maurya, Founder of thehitchedstories

Our Philosophy

How We Work

Authentic Moments

We believe the most beautiful photographs happen naturally. We capture real emotions, genuine interactions, and candid moments that tell your true story.

Editorial Style

Our photographs have a timeless, cinematic quality—like pages from a beautiful magazine, but filled with the genuine emotions of your special day.

Storytelling Focus

Every wedding tells a unique story. We weave together the details, emotions, and moments into a visual narrative you'll treasure forever.

Personal Connection

We take time to know our couples before the wedding day. This connection allows us to anticipate moments and capture your personalities authentically.
